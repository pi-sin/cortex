# Skill: Pricing Analysis

When thinking about monetization or pricing:

## 1. Competitive Scan

List 5 comparable products: | Product | Free Tier | Pro Price | Team Price | What's Gated |

## 2. Value Metric

What unit to charge on? Evaluate: per user/month, per integration, per AI query, flat fee.
Pick the one that aligns with how users perceive value.

## 3. Tier Design

- **Free:** Enough to get hooked (2-3 integrations, unified inbox, basic widgets, no AI)
- **Pro ($X/mo):** The "I need this" tier (unlimited integrations, AI features, cross-app search)
- **Team ($X/user/mo):** Shared dashboards, admin controls, priority support

## 4. Psychology

- Penny gap: free → $1 is harder than $1 → $10
- "Oh no" moment on downgrade: which feature can't they live without?
- Annual discount: 20-30% to drive commitment

## 5. Revenue Model

At X users × Y% conversion × $Z/mo = MRR
Show pessimistic, realistic, and optimistic scenarios.
